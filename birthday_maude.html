⛏️ HOW TO PLAY ⛏️

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Click blocks to build COMBOS and earn the highest score before time runs out!

📊 BLOCK TYPES & POINTS

⬜ Stone: 10 pts (common)
✨ Gold: 30 pts (medium)
💎 Diamond: 50 pts (rare)
🟢 Emerald: 40 pts (rare)
💣 TNT: 200 pts (VERY RARE - clears area!)
💚 Creeper: -50 pts (avoid these!)

⛓️ COMBO SYSTEM

Click the same block type multiple times to build a COMBO!

• Click Gold → +30 pts
• Click Gold again → +34 pts (1.15x)
• Click Gold again → +40 pts (1.3x)
• Total: 104 pts for 3 blocks!

When you click a DIFFERENT block type, your combo ends and you get all the points!

⚡ SPECIAL BLOCKS

💣 TNT (VERY RARE): Click for 200 pts + clears nearby blocks!

💚 Creeper (FREQUENT): Hit by accident = lose 50 pts! Watch out!

🎮 DIFFICULTY MODES

⭐ NORMAL: 30 seconds, relax and learn
🔥 HARD: 45 seconds, more enemies!
💀 INSANE: 60 seconds, extreme speed!

💡 PRO TIPS

• Build long combos for massive multipliers!
• Avoid Creepers - they cost 50 points!
• Grab TNT when you see it - worth 200 pts!
• Game speeds up every 10 seconds!
• Strategy beats speed - choose wisely!
